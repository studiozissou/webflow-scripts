- **Missing meta descriptions:** 3 → 0. Same store pages now have descriptions.
- **Duplicate meta descriptions:** 50 → 38. Improved.
- **Incorrect pages in sitemap:** 43 → 9. Major cleanup.
- **Slow page load:** 4,032 → 3,882. Slight improvement.
- **Orphaned pages:** 93 → 86. -7.

### Regressed
- **5xx errors:** 0 → 2. Two VDP pages returning server errors:
  - /vehicles/used/fl68wfu
  - /vehicles/used/yf75kyv
- **Broken internal links:** 10 → 22. Increased — new model pages (Renault, BMW 1 Series, Renault 5 e-Tech Electric) plus 19 VDPs with broken cross-references.
- **Structured data errors:** 1,235 → 1,299. Slightly worse. Now includes:
  - 47 make pages (FAQ empty slots)
  - 449 model pages (FAQ empty slots)
  - 803 VDP pages (Product/Vehicle schema errors)

- **Pages not crawled:** 36 → 100. +64, all VDP pages.
- **Duplicate content:** 759 → 862. +103, inventory growth.
- **Duplicate titles:** 1,950 → 2,015. +65, inventory growth.
- **Multiple H1 tags:** 2 → 3. /terms/data-privacy is new.

### Unchanged

- Nofollow internal links: still 25 across 19 pages (7 fuel + 11 sell-car + /sell-car/value-car)
