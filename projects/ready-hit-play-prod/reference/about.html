LEADERSHIP TEAM

Ryan Crisman

Founder/CEO

Ryan Crisman is a storyteller at the intersection of culture, business, and belief, and the founder of Ready Hit Play. His work spans feature films, global brand campaigns, and executive storytelling for C-suites of global brands, as well as emerging leaders in space and innovation. With a background in long-form interviewing and broadcast storytelling, Ryan has a rare ability to draw out clarity, conviction, and narrative truth from the people building what’s next. He brings that precision to moments where credibility, clarity, and cultural impact matter most.

Fun fact: Ryan’s long-form interviews for Dan Aykroyd’s House of Blues Radio Hour are now part of the U.S. Library of Congress.
