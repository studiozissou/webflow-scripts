## TL;DR

**Positioning:** "Website valet." Retainer-focused service site. Three pillars: Design & Build, Automate & Integrate, Manage & Grow. Webflow is one tool, not the whole offering.

**Core insight (implicit in copy, never explicit):** Clients get sites built by big agencies then get handed to a junior PM. They want a senior contact with deep expertise who executes fast. The copy shows what we offer — the reader fills in the contrast.

**Visual direction:** Blend Teenage Engineering OP-1 + Ghibli + Nazca geoglyphs + ligne claire. Signal orange accent in a bone/clay palette. Illustration system of friendly submarine/console/topographic elements. Decorative pipes, portholes, cable runs.

**Charm strategy:** Technical message + playful visuals. The tension is the brand — don't resolve it.

## Audiences

- **Primary:** End clients with existing agency-built sites, frustrated with aftercare, $3-10k/mo retainer budget
- **Secondary:** Agencies needing a reliable embedded partner (served via dedicated strip)
