    uid=3_107 StaticText "RailOS separates the safety-critical ETCS application layer from the hardware device layer. When a Baseline is updated we simply update our software, the underlying hardware remains untouched. RailOS was designed for low-cost and software-only adaptation to the ever evolving ETCS standard."
