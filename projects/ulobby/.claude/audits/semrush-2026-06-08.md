## Errors (221 total)

### 171 malformed links (Cookiebot `javascript:` protocol)
- **Root cause:** Cookiebot consent widget injects `href="javascript:Cookiebot.renew()"` site-wide
- Crawlers resolve this as `https://javascript:Cookiebot.renew()` — inflates error count
- Affects all 171 crawled pages
- **Fix:** Replace `<a href="javascript:...">` with `<button onclick="Cookiebot.renew()">`

### 22 structured data markup errors (all SOFTWARE_APP type)
- All 22 errors are on Solution pages across all 4 locales (EN, DA, NO, SV)
- Missing required fields: `aggregateRating` or `review`, and `offers`
- Homepage variants (EN, DA, NO, SV) also have `contactPoint` not recognized on SOFTWARE_APP
- **Affected pages:** `/solutions/*` and `/` across all locales
- **Fix:** Either add required fields or change schema type (SoftwareApplication requires offers+review per Google spec). Consider switching to `WebApplication` or `Product` with appropriate fields.

### 12 duplicate title tags
- All on Blog listing pages across locales + paginated variants
- All share title "Insights | Ulobby"
- `/blog`, `/da/blog`, `/no/blogg`, `/sv/blog` + `?eb3f9b85_page=1`, `?eb3f9b85_page=2`
- **Fix:** Add page number to title on paginated pages, translate title per locale

### 12 duplicate meta descriptions
- Same blog listing pages as duplicate titles
- **Fix:** Unique descriptions per locale, append page numbers on paginated pages

### 4 hreflang conflicts within page source code
- Conflicts detected on 4 pages
- **Fix:** Audit hreflang tags for consistency across locale variants
