The Signalling Company could not be prouder to announce the certification of its on-board ETCS safety system and its adoption by Škoda Group for all its new single-deck train platform and retrofit projects.

READ THE PRESS RELEASE

Lighter, simpler, smaller, faster to install

These attributes of our safety platform, and others like its energy efficiency, combine to make it both an optimal solution for new trains and a viable one for even the most challenging retrofit projects.

This value was recognised early by Lineas, Europe’s largest freight fleet operator. Speaking on behalf of Lineas, Bruno Vanlede (Head of Rail Fleet Management), reflected that “Our seed investment in TSC was driven by our need for a more financially sustainable ETCS solution to further improve the safety of our operations and continue supporting the modal shift. It was clear to us from the start that TSC’s vision was aligned with ours, and they had the technology keys to make it happen. We are delighted for the team and this Belgian success story.”

From 2019 to ETCS certification today

Alexandre Betis, CEO at TSC, shared his pride, noting “ETCS Certification is the Everest of the safety and signalling world. I want to take this moment of great pride to thank our incredible team, without their phenomenal dedication none of this would have been possible. When Stan and I [Stanislas Pinte, TSC co-founder] set out on this journey we came to appreciate how much the industry was willing us to succeed. This is the moment we begin to repay the faith that Lineas, Škoda Group, and others have placed in us.”
