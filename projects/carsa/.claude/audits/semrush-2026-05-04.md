## Key Findings

### Slow Page Load Speed (15 pages)

Store pages with maps and vehicle listing pages with filter parameters:
- /stores/bradford, /stores/gloucester, /stores/towcester
- /used-cars?cars_full-price_less-equal=7500 (and 4 more filter variants)
- /vehicles/used/ak71bww, de72zbf, ov70uua, pl23ryn, sa70oul, wv72szd

**Likely causes:** Mapbox GL rendering on store pages; large JS bundles (VWO, n8n Chat, JetBoost) on vehicle pages; image-heavy vehicle detail pages.

### Links Lead to HTTP (3 pages — terms section)

- /terms/data-privacy
- /terms/terms-conditions
- /terms/vehicle-purchase

These likely contain hardcoded `http://` links in CMS rich text fields. Quick fix: find and replace in CMS content.

### Duplicate Meta Descriptions (2 pages)

- /part-exchange and /value-car share the same meta description

These are distinct pages (part-exchange vs sell outright) and need unique descriptions.

### Missing H1 (1 page)

- /reserve — has no H1 tag at all

### Links with No Anchor Text (94 pages, 109 instances)

Sitewide issue — likely caused by icon-only links (social media, logo link, image links without text). This correlates with our accessibility audit finding (icon links lacking aria-labels).

### Title Too Long (2 pages)

- /car-care/carsacover
- /vehicles/used/ak71bww (dynamic title from CMS fields may concatenate too long)
