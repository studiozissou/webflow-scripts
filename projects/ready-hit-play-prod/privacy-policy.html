The short version

We collect what we need to run the site and nothing more. No selling your data, no shady third parties, no surprises.

Who we are

Ready Hit Play is a creative studio based in Amsterdam. This policy covers readyhitplay.com.

What we collect

When you visit the site:

  • Basic analytics (pages visited, time on site, device type, rough location by country) via Google Analytics 4
  • No personal identifiers unless you give them to us

When you get in touch:

  • Whatever you put in the contact form — name, email, your message
  • We use this to reply to you. That's it.

Cookies

We use cookies to understand how the site performs and what people actually look at. You can opt out through the cookie banner when you first visit.

Essential cookies

Keep the site functioning (Webflow session, form handling). These are always on.

Analytics cookies

Google Analytics 4 — anonymised usage data so we can see what's landing. These are optional.

Google Analytics uses first-party cookies (_ga, _ga_*) with a default retention of 14 months. IP addresses are anonymised before processing. We don't enable Google Signals, User-ID, or advertising features.

Who sees your data

  • Us. The team at Ready Hit Play.
  • Webflow. Our site is hosted on Webflow. They process data under their own privacy policy.
  • Google Analytics. Anonymised usage data only, processed under Google's data processing terms.

We don't sell, rent, or trade your information. Ever.

Your rights

You're based in the EU or visiting from it, so GDPR applies. You can:

  • Ask what data we have on you
  • Ask us to delete it
  • Withdraw consent for analytics cookies anytime
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

Drop us a line and we'll sort it out within 30 days.

Data retention

  • Contact form submissions: kept for as long as the conversation is active, then deleted.
  • Analytics data: 14 months, anonymised and aggregated — no personal records stored.

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll note the date at the top. No buried changes.
