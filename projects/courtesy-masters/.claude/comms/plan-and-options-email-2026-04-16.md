Hi Menno-Paul,

The audit is wrapped. Below is the full plan split into four priority bands — **P0-P3 run as a fixed-scope Phase 0 over roughly three weeks, and P4 sits on a monthly retainer** because it's discovery-led and content-driven. All work is billed at €120/hour.

---

### P0 — Week 1 — Easy wins + signal-quality

**Top tasks**

- Shorten 27 truncated page titles (direct SERP CTR uplift)
- Fix 3 duplicate H1s on insights posts, hreflang typo on `/jobs`, aria-labels on 5 icon-only links
- Image + font optimisation — 80% of bandwidth today is oversized PNGs (LCP drops to <1.2s on `/for-employers`)
- Clean 28 pages with broken outbound links, fix Cookiebot contrast, unpublish 7 dev/test pages
- File the Webflow Support ticket to remove the partnercode (stops Egenix earning an ongoing affiliate commission on your hosting)

**Wins:** SEO, trust, accessibility, performance, GDPR alignment — all visible in the first sprint.

**Pricing:** ~6-7 hours → **€720 – €840**

---

### P1 — Week 1 — Critical de-risk + core schema

**Top tasks**

- Migrate the Egenix custom JS to a client-owned GitHub repo *(this is the single biggest site-continuity risk today — the code currently lives on the previous developer's GitHub)*
- Clean up duplicate Lenis and align GSAP/ScrollTrigger versions (handled inside the migration)
- JobPosting schema on the 19 jobs missing it — unlocks Google for Jobs eligibility
- Replace Webflow's default "Product snippet" with a proper Organization schema site-wide

**Wins:** Removes the #1 business-continuity risk and unlocks Google for Jobs on 19 more listings.

**Pricing:** ~6-7 hours → **€720 – €840**

---

### P2 — Week 2 — Rich results + client-visible design

**Top tasks**

- Kick-ass footer redesign — brief plus 2 Figma directions for sign-off
- URL structure rewrite — `/insights/news/`, `/insights/blog/`, `/insights/video/` with a complete 301 redirect map
- FAQPage schema on 30 FAQs, Article schema on 16 insights + 3 case studies, BreadcrumbList schema as static HTML
- Padding and spacing consistency pass on the 5 top pages (pixelperfect)

**Wins:** The visible wins you've been asking for, plus the schema trio that unlocks rich results in Google and citations in ChatGPT / Perplexity / SGE.

**Pricing:** ~13-16 hours → **€1,560 – €1,920**

---

### P3 — Week 2-3 — Editorial foundation + operational stability

**Top tasks**

- Sitewide typography and layout standards — center column under the mega header, 1.5 line-height minimum, left-align body text, `hyphens: none` for Dutch, bullet/list token styling
- Body text colour demo — 2-3 values around 75-85% grey on staging, you pick the one that feels right
- Form simplification — consolidate 12 contact sub-pages into one form per audience with a topic dropdown
- Replace Zapier + reCAPTCHA with Klaviyo API + Microsoft Power Automate + Webflow's built-in AI spam filter
- CMS field explainers, CSS documentation, auto-generated architecture diagram, Webflow billing walkthrough

**Wins:** The site looks, reads, and runs the way you want. Editors can work independently without a manual. Two third-party dependencies gone.

**Pricing:** ~19-29 hours → **€2,280 – €3,480**

---

### Phase 0 total (P0 – P3)

**~44-59 hours over ~3 weeks → €5,280 – €7,080**

Paid 30% deposit / 40% mid-project / 30% on delivery.

- AEO remediation from L1 (invisible) to L3 (competitive) — answer-first copy, author bylines, authority signals
- Candidate and employer funnel automation — discovery session first, then Powerpath / Dynamics integration
- Klaviyo end-to-end — segmented marketing flows + transactional email
- AI agents (human-in-the-loop) — SEO monitoring, content creation, brand-voice check

After the 6-month minimum, it's month-to-month with 30 days' notice.
