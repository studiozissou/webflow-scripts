### A2. Ervaringen — section heading mismatch — P1

- **Design:** "Herken jij jezelf hierin?" + subtitle about real stories
- **Live:** "Ervaringen met NEM Life" + different subtitle
- The design heading is warmer and more inviting per brand voice.

**How:** Select the H2 element on `/ervaringen` and update text to
"Herken jij jezelf hierin?". Update the subtitle paragraph to match
the design copy.

---

### A3. Link-in-Bio (Christel) — article cards 2-col vs stacked — P1

- **Design:** Article cards under "NEM Methode" and "NEM Life" are
  full-width, stacked vertically (one card per row)
- **Live:** Cards display in a 2-column grid, making them very small
  at 390px. Text is cramped and thumbnails are tiny.

**How:** On the card grid wrapper at the `small` breakpoint, change
`grid-template-columns` from `1fr 1fr` to `1fr` (single column).
Cards become full-width and readable.

---

### A4. NEM Methode — yellow card sections tight spacing — P2

The numbered 123 cards and the "Waarom anders" comparison cards have
minimal vertical padding between card groups on mobile. Design shows
more breathing room between sections (approx 48-64px, live looks approx
16-24px).

**How:** Increase `margin-bottom` or `padding-bottom` on the card section
wrappers at the `small` breakpoint. Target 48-64px gap between sections.

---

### A5. Footer — placeholder `#` links — P2

Multiple footer links point to `#` (placeholder):
- Thema's, Populair, Zelftest 1, Zelftest 2, Over NEM Life, Onze missie,
  Alex, Contact, Nieuwsbrief NEM Matters, NEM WISE, Therapie,
  Relatietherapie, Via Puur, Privacy, YouTube, LinkedIn, Instagram

**How:** Update links where the destination page exists on staging.
Leave links to pages that don't exist yet as `#`. Don't hide any links.

Known destinations:
- NEM Methode → `/nem-methode`
- Christel → `/link-in-bio/christel`
- Voorwaarden → `/voorwaarden`
