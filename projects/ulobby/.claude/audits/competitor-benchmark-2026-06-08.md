**Date:** 2026-06-08

## 1. Executive Summary

Ulobby operates in a low-volume, high-intent niche: Public Affairs SaaS. In Denmark, the competitive landscape is fragmented -- most "competitors" share only 1 common keyword. In the US, the field is more developed with dedicated advocacy/lobbying software vendors competing on product-category terms.

Key findings:
- Ulobby's DK presence (15 keywords, ~89 traffic) is small but stable. No DK competitor dominates the public affairs software space.
- The US market shows stronger competitive pressure. CiviClick (938 keywords, 758 traffic) and PublicAffairsNetworking.com (337 keywords, 1,161 traffic) are the primary organic threats.
- CiviClick is on a steep growth trajectory -- keywords up 2.7x in 12 months (340 to 905) -- and is capturing the "advocacy software" category that Ulobby could own.
- Ulobby's US keyword count has grown from 31 to 70 over 12 months, but traffic remains negligible (6/mo). The content is ranking but not converting to clicks.

## 8. Strategic Recommendations

### Immediate (0-3 months)

1. **Create a "Lobbying Software" product category page** targeting "lobbying software", "advocacy software", and "public affairs software". This is the highest-value gap -- CiviClick owns this space and Ulobby has no dedicated landing page for it.

2. **Publish Danish-language content on "lobbyisme"** (880 vol). No competitor ranks well for this term. A definitive guide ("Hvad er lobbyisme? En komplet guide") could capture significant DK traffic.

3. **Optimise existing blog post** "7 Important Roles of the Public Affairs Department" -- it ranks #19 for "public affairs" (4,400 vol US). Improving on-page SEO, internal linking, and content depth could push it to page 1.

### Short-term (3-6 months)

4. **Build a "What is Public Affairs" pillar page** for the US market. PAN's content is dated (static .php pages). A modern, comprehensive resource could compete. Combined volume of definitional queries: ~3,500/mo.

5. **Target "stakeholder management" in DK** more aggressively. Ulobby ranks #11 for "stakeholder management dansk" (90 vol) and #36 for "stakeholder mapping" (590 vol). Dedicated landing pages and Danish-language blog content could push both into top 5.

6. **Create comparison content**: "Ulobby vs CiviClick", "Best Public Affairs Software 2026". CiviClick already ranks for "best digital advocacy platforms 2025" -- Ulobby should own the 2026 version.

### Medium-term (6-12 months)

7. **Expand into "grassroots" keyword cluster.** CiviClick dominates but the terms are low-difficulty. Target: "grassroots advocacy software", "grassroots mobilization tool", "grassroots campaigning platform".

8. **Build topical authority in the US** through consistent publication on political monitoring, stakeholder management, and government affairs topics. Ulobby's US keyword count is growing (31 to 70 in 12 months) but traffic is near zero because positions are too low. More content + internal linking will push existing rankings up.

9. **Consider UK market entry.** PAN gets 1,630 traffic from UK with 714 keywords. No strong UK-specific public affairs software competitor exists. Ulobby's EU positioning could be an advantage.
