The CEO of Lineas, Geert Pauwels, won the European Railway Award. This is an important step for the sector, as Geert is the first CEO of a railfreight company to ever win this prize.

Among all modes of transportations, trains offer a unique combination of environmental and capacity efficiency. In the coming battle against climate change, they can offer a great contribution, especially if they can circulate across Europe as easily as road trucks or buses currently do.

The Signalling Company, a joint venture between ERTMS Solutions and Lineas, was created to address these challenges. It starts by providing a competitive ETCS onboard solution.
