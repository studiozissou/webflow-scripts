# Deep Research Prompt — Zissou Archive Networking & Market Intelligence

Copy the prompt below into Claude Research (or a new Claude conversation with web search enabled).

---

## PROMPT

I'm launching a curated vintage menswear business called Zissou Archive, based in Den Haag, Netherlands. I sell online via Vinted, Grailed, eBay, and eventually my own website. My focus is heritage menswear: Italian and British tailoring, Japanese denim, quality outerwear (Barbour, Crombie, Grenfell), and accessories — the kind of pieces featured on Permanent Style but at accessible, pre-owned prices.

I need deep, actionable research across the areas below. For every store, person, or organisation you find, give me: **name, location, website, Instagram handle, email or contact method (if publicly available), and a one-line note on why they're relevant.** I'll use this as a contact list and outreach database.

### 1. Vintage & menswear stores in the Netherlands

Map the full landscape. I want to know who's doing what, where the gaps are, and who I should visit or build relationships with.

**Den Haag:**
- All vintage clothing stores (not just the well-known ones)
- All menswear-focused shops (new or vintage)
- High-end consignment/second-hand shops
- Japanese denim or workwear specialists (I know about Store du Nord — who else?)
- Tailors and alteration specialists
- Cobblers who do Goodyear welt resoling
- Dry cleaners who handle delicate/vintage fabrics
- Leather repair specialists

**Amsterdam:**
- Same categories as above
- Specifically: stores that stock Japanese brands (Kapital, Beams, etc.)
- Vintage stores with a menswear focus or strong menswear section
- Any "curated" vintage shops (not kilo-sale bulk)

**Other NL cities (Rotterdam, Utrecht, Leiden, Haarlem, Groningen):**
- Notable vintage stores worth visiting for sourcing or inspiration
- Any menswear specialists

### 2. Key people in Dutch menswear / vintage clothing

I want to know the people, not just the shops. Who are the tastemakers, shop owners, bloggers, YouTubers, and community builders in the NL menswear and vintage space?

- Dutch menswear bloggers, YouTubers, or Instagram accounts with 1,000+ followers
- Shop owners who are publicly active (Instagram, interviews, podcast appearances)
- Dutch contributors to Permanent Style, Styleforum, or similar forums
- Anyone running menswear events, pop-ups, or trunk shows in NL
- Dutch vintage clothing dealers with a strong online presence (Vinted, Depop, Grailed, eBay)
- Makers and artisans: people making leather goods, accessories, or clothing in NL

### 3. Events, fairs, and markets

- All recurring vintage/flea markets in the Randstad (Den Haag, Amsterdam, Rotterdam, Utrecht)
- Fashion trade fairs in NL (Modefabriek, etc.) — dates, how to attend, cost
- Any menswear-specific events (trunk shows, brand launches, pop-ups)
- Sustainability/circular fashion events in NL
- Craft/maker markets where I could sell or network

### 4. Dutch vintage/resale industry contacts

- Trade associations or industry groups for second-hand retailers in NL
- Branchevereniging Kringloopbedrijven Nederland (BKN) — how to engage
- Any Dutch reseller communities (Facebook groups, Discord servers, Telegram groups)
- Wholesale vintage suppliers in NL or nearby (Belgium, Germany) who sell bales/lots

### 5. International contacts for sourcing and selling

**Japan (key export market):**
- Japanese vintage dealers or shops who buy European vintage (especially Barbour, military, tailoring)
- Hollywood Ranch Market — contact info, how they source from Europe
- Japanese proxy buying services (Buyee, ZenMarket, FromJapan) — which is best for buying Japanese brands to resell in Europe?
- Any Japanese menswear community figures who engage with European sellers

**UK (key sourcing market):**
- Barbour rewaxing services — full list with prices, turnaround times, and reviews
- UK charity shops known for quality menswear (Oxfam boutiques, Sue Ryder, etc.)
- UK-based vintage dealers who sell wholesale or in bulk
- UK menswear bloggers/influencers who might collaborate or cross-promote

**US (key export market):**
- Grailed power sellers in menswear — who are the top accounts to study?
- US vintage menswear community figures (Instagram, YouTube)
- US-based customers who buy European vintage — where do they hang out online?

### 6. Potential partnerships and collaborations

For each, explain why the partnership makes sense and suggest an approach:
- Coffee shops or bars in Den Haag that might host a pop-up clothing rail
- Creative spaces or co-working spaces in Den Haag suitable for a showroom/photography studio
- Local photographers who do product/fashion photography (TFP or affordable rates)
- KABK (Royal Academy of Art, Den Haag) fashion department — how to connect with students for model/styling work
- Film and TV production companies in the Randstad — how to become a costume supplier
- Sustainable fashion organisations in NL

### 7. Competitor analysis

For each competitor, give me: what they do well, what they do poorly, their price range, their audience, and what I can learn from them.

- Online vintage menswear sellers based in NL (Vinted, Depop, Grailed, eBay stores)
- Curated vintage menswear websites anywhere in Europe (e.g. VintageFolk UK, Messina Hembry)
- Anyone in NL doing what I'm planning (Permanent Style-adjacent curated vintage)

### Output format

Please organise all findings into tables with these columns:
| Name | Location | Website | Instagram | Contact Method | Category | Why Relevant |

Group by section (stores, people, events, etc.). Include direct links wherever possible. If you can't find an email, note the best alternative contact method (Instagram DM, website contact form, etc.).

At the end, give me your **top 10 people I should contact first** — prioritised by how useful the relationship would be for a new vintage menswear seller in Den Haag, with a suggested outreach message for each.
