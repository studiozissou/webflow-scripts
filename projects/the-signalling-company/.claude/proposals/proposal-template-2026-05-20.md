## How it works

### 1. Template selection

I shortlist 2-3 Webflow templates that fit TSC's structure (product pages, news, contact). We pick one together. I'll flag what works out of the box and what needs adjustment.

### 2. Branding and content integration

I apply TSC's brand (logo, colours, fonts, imagery) across the template and drop in your page content. All 14 pages. The template's layout stays mostly intact. I'll adjust spacing, typography, and component styling to match your brand, but I won't redesign sections from scratch.

### 3. CMS setup

Insights & News collection with categories and tags, ready for your team to publish articles on their own.

### 4. Gated document downloads

Datasheets and brochures can sit behind a form. Visitors fill in their details before they get the download link. Webflow handles this natively, no custom code. Form submissions are stored in Webflow and can be exported or connected to your CRM later.

### 5. Fine-tuning and QA

Responsive testing across devices and browsers. SEO basics (performance, metadata, heading structure, alt text). Accessibility basics (WCAG AA colour contrast, semantic markup). Contact form setup. One review round with your team.

### 6. Handover and training

Site transferred to TSC's own Webflow account. I do a walkthrough session so your team knows how to edit content, publish news, and manage the site going forward.

---

## Your team manages the site after handover

Webflow is built for this. After handover, your team can:

- Add new product pages. Duplicate an existing one, update the content, publish. No code.
- Change text, images, and features on any page using the visual editor.
- Publish news and insights through the CMS. Add articles, assign categories and tags, publish whenever you want.
- Gate or un-gate documents by editing the download sections.
- Update contact forms and other page elements.

The walkthrough covers all of this. I also include written guidance for the most common tasks.
