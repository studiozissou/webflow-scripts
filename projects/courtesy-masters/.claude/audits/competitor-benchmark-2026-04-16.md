**Scope:** Organic visibility, keyword profile, traffic concentration, content signals
**Related:** `reports/intake-report-2026-04-15.md`, `audits/seo.md`, `audits/aeo.md`

---

## Headline

CourtesyMasters sits at the **bottom of its own declared competitor set** on every headline organic metric except domain authority rank (where it is marginally ahead of 2 of the 4). The gap to `hospitality-group.nl` is ~10× in keyword count and ~21× in traffic — but that competitor is effectively a **different business** (hospitality consultancy + workplace design, not pure executive search), so the most useful benchmarks are the three pure-play recruitment firms: `mjpeople.nl`, `independenthospitality.nl`, and `thehospitalityrecruiters.com`. Against those, CM's gap is narrower — mostly a keyword-efficiency and on-page-authority problem, not a structural one.

## 5. Content-pattern comparison

| Domain | Service-level landing pages | Job detail pages ranking | Content (articles/insights) | Notes |
|--------|----------------------------|--------------------------|-----------------------------|-------|
| mjpeople.nl | **Yes — strong** (GM, hotel professionals) | Yes | "Structuur van een sollicitatiegesprek" ranks | Biggest content footprint of the pure-plays |
| hospitality-group.nl | Yes (expertises/interim recruitment, projects) | No | Project case studies rank | Consultancy model, not directly comparable |
| independenthospitality.nl | Minimal | Yes (vacancy pages) | Thin | Homepage-heavy + vacancy pages only |
| thehospitalityrecruiters.com | Minimal | No | Thin | 7 URLs total in index — hyper-focused |
| **courtesymasters.com** | **Minimal** | Yes (3 job pages rank) | **19 insights posts** — but only 3 rank | Content exists but isn't ranking |

**Takeaway:** CM has **more existing content** than 3/4 competitors (19 insights, 6 case studies, 22 job listings), but that content isn't being indexed/weighted because of:

- Inconsistent schema (only 3/22 jobs have JobPosting, 3/19 insights have Article — see `audits/seo.md` §1.2)
- No author bylines, no updated timestamps, no internal linking density (AEO blockers)
- 27 overly long page titles that get truncated in SERPs
- No service-level landing pages (e.g. "Hospitality General Manager Recruitment", "F&B Director Recruitment") — CM goes straight from homepage to job listings with nothing in between

**The `mjpeople.nl` model** — a handful of deep, role-specific landing pages (general manager, hotel professionals) layered over the jobs list — is the **most directly transferable pattern** for CM.

| Traffic/KW efficiency | 0.50 | 0.8-1.0 | Better position on higher-volume keywords (matches `independenthospitality.nl` today) |
| Traffic cost ($ equiv.) | $42 | $150-250 | Rank for more commercial-intent terms (currently over-indexed on branded/info) |
| Top page concentration | 66% | 50-55% | Service-level landing pages to diversify, mirror `mjpeople.nl` |

These targets are **competitive parity with the pure-play recruiter cohort** (mjpeople, independenthospitality, thr), not the outlier `hospitality-group.nl`. Parity is achievable with the schema + content + technical remediation already scoped in Option 1 + Option 2 of the proposal — it does not require a brand-new content programme.

## 7. Strategic recommendations

1. **Defend the Dutch-language "horeca recruitment" cluster** — these are CM's crown jewels (pos #2-4 on 50-90 vol keywords). Add FAQPage schema on the NL homepage sections that discuss horeca recruitment. Add Organization + HiringOrganization schema to signal relevance.
2. **Close the English "hospitality recruitment / headhunter / recruiter" gap** — build 3-5 role-specific landing pages (General Manager, F&B Director, Revenue Manager, Personal Assistant UHNWI, Front Office Director) that can rank independently. Copy the `mjpeople.nl` structural pattern.
3. **Get job detail pages eligible for Google for Jobs** — 19/22 jobs still missing JobPosting schema. This is the highest-ROI, lowest-effort move on the site.
4. **Get insights posts ranking** — only 3/19 have Article schema. Add to all 19, plus author bylines, dated updates, and 2-3 internal links per post to service/industry pages. Watch for indexation uplift at 8-12 weeks.
5. **Title optimisation** — 27 pages have titles >60 chars. Truncation in SERPs depresses CTR by ~20-30%. Fix at CMS template level.
6. **De-prioritise direct comparison with `hospitality-group.nl`** — their business is different (consultancy + workplace design), their traffic comes primarily from a single head term ("hospitality"), and catching them requires a content programme an order of magnitude larger than CM's scope.
