The short version

Who we are

Ready Hit Play is a creative agency based in Amsterdam. This policy covers readyhitplay.com.

What we collect

Cookies

We use cookies to understand how the site performs and what people actually look at. You can opt out through the cookie banner when you first visit.

Essential cookies keep the site working. These are always on.
Analytics cookies help us see what's landing. These are optional.

We don't sell, rent, or trade your information. Ever.

Your rights

You're based in the EU or visiting from it, so GDPR applies. You can:

  • Ask what data we have on you

Drop us a line and we'll sort it out.

Data retention

Analytics data: anonymised and aggregated — no personal records stored.

Changes
