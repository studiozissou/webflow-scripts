# Inline Comments for TSC Copy Doc

12 comments to add directly in the shared Word doc.

1. **Customers page — Why/How/What sections are identical across all 4 segments.** Each ICP has different concerns — fleet operators care about downtime, leasing companies care about asset value, OEMs care about integration. Tailor 2-3 sentences per segment.
2. **Products page — ETCS section.** "Optimized capex and lifetime opex" is the right message but needs a concrete proof point. Add a number or comparison.
3. **Products page — PZB/KVB/TBL1+ descriptions are nearly identical.** Differentiate by leading with the specific country/network context and what makes each unique.
4. **Services — Homologation.** Huge differentiator. Expand with a brief description of what this actually involves — from initial design through to getting authorisation from the national safety authority. Buyers need to understand that TSC manages the entire approval process, not just supplies the equipment.
5. **Services — Training.** Worth expanding — who gets trained? Drivers, depot technicians, operational managers? Shows commitment beyond the sale.
6. **About page — Missing "why TSC" narrative.** Add founding team credentials (where they worked before TSC), the Skoda Group affiliation, and the "we entrust children's lives to our systems" line from the values deck.
7. **Case studies — Missing outcome metrics.** For Lineas: average installation time per loco? Uptime during rollout? Fleet availability? Even qualitative outcomes help.
8. **Case studies — No customer quotes.** Even one sentence from a Lineas project manager adds enormous credibility. Worth requesting.
9. **Open RailOS — Application form.** Budget questions ("What budget do you have for the ADK?") feel premature for first contact. Simplify to: describe your app, which ADK, timeline, open to co-development.
10. **RailOS Devices — No hierarchy.** A system diagram showing how devices connect would be worth 1,000 words. Which are core vs optional? Which come bundled with products?
11. **Homepage — Still TBD.** Most critical page. Prioritise this week. Need: hero copy, 3 value propositions, trust bar (logos), featured case study.
12. **FAQ page — Great addition.** Structure as a CMS collection filtered by page/category so FAQs can appear contextually on product pages too.
