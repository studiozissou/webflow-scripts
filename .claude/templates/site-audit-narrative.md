## The three-beat story

### 1. Where the site is today

Synthesize from `reports/intake-report-YYYY-MM-DD.md`:

- **Foundation is solid.** Pull the key "Passing" stats — meta coverage, alt text, SSL,
  analytics, sitemap, etc. Make them concrete (X/Y pages, 0/N missing).
- **The brand already competes.** Pull any existing search rankings, backlinks, or traffic
  that show the brand has authority. If SEMRush data is available, cite the #N positions
  on money keywords.
- **The gap is narrower than it looks.** If competitor benchmark data exists, reframe the
  competitive position — show that against the real peer set (not the outlier leader),
  the client is within striking distance.

### 2. What's holding it back

Synthesize the "Needs Attention" and "Missing or Broken" sections into 4-6 plain-language
problems. Each should be:
- Concrete and countable ("19 of 22 jobs are invisible to Google for Jobs")
- Linked to business impact ("those 3 that have it rank; the other 19 don't")
- Non-catastrophic framing ("none of these is a crisis; together they cap visibility")

Common patterns to look for:
- Schema gaps (JobPosting, FAQPage, Article, Person, Organization, BreadcrumbList)
- Truncated page titles costing SERP CTR
- AI platform invisibility (low AEO score)
- Performance/bandwidth issues (oversized images)
- Third-party code dependencies (business-critical risk)
- CMS clutter (dev pages, thin template pages)
- Missing role-specific landing pages

### 3. What the work unlocks

Frame the remediation as 3 pillars:

**Pillar 1 — De-risk.** Remove dependencies, fix code hygiene, clean CMS clutter.
Insurance against a silent break.

**Pillar 2 — Unlock search + AI visibility.** Schema expansion, title fixes, heading
cleanup, landing pages. Google rich results, Google for Jobs, AI search citations —
all become possible.

**Pillar 3 — Grow.** Content programme, competitive gap analysis, international SEO,
AEO remediation. Compound what Pillars 1-2 build.

---

## Why this framing matters

Three points:
1. **Gives the client credit** for the quality that's already there.
2. **Makes the gap concrete and countable** rather than abstract.
3. **Makes the ROI vivid** — specific, measurable outcomes from the work.

Generate 5-7 short phrases that capture the narrative. These become the shared language

Tailor these to the specific client's situation and industry language.
