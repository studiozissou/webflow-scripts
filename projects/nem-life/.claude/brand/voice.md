# Brand Voice — NEM Life

## Tone Ladder
Formal ←——○———→ Casual: Centre-casual (warm professional, never stiff)
Technical ←———○——→ Accessible: Accessible-leaning (psychology concepts made tangible)
Serious ←——○———→ Playful: Centre-serious (grounded, never heavy or clinical)

## Vocabulary

### Always use
- "doorbreken" (break through) — core brand action, appears in slogan
- "patronen" (patterns) — central concept; behaviour patterns, not abstract theory
- "helderheid" / "clarity" — brand promise; clarity over clutter
- "aanpak" (approach) — practical, structured; not "methode" alone
- "stap" (step) — implies progress, forward movement
- "concreet" (concrete/tangible) — grounded, not abstract
- Direct "je/jij" address — warm, personal second person

### Never use
- "therapie-jargon" — no DSM labels, no clinical shorthand
- "healing journey" or English coaching buzzwords — stay Dutch, stay grounded
- "magisch" / "transformatie" — no hype, no spiritual language
- "eindeloos praten" (endless talking) — explicitly positioned against in briefing
- Dramatic superlatives ("revolutionair", "uniek") — calm confidence, not hype

## Example Sentences
> "Je bent niet het probleem. Je mist alleen een heldere aanpak."
> "Doorbreek het patroon dat je steeds weer terugtrekt."
> "Concreet, stapsgewijs en direct toepasbaar. Zonder eindeloos praten."

## Content Principles
1. **Clarity over clutter** — every sentence earns its place; no filler, no padding
2. **Structure over overload** — present information in steps, pillars, numbered sequences
3. **Direction over reflection-only** — always point forward; acknowledge the problem, then show the path
4. **Warmth without softness** — empathetic but confident; "I see you" not "poor you"
