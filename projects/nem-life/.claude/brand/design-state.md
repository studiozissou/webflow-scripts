# Design State — NEM Life

## Visual Spirit
Calm, grounded, and structured. The design creates space — generous whitespace, clear hierarchy, no visual noise. Photography is warm and natural (outdoor scenes, real people, golden light). The overall feeling is "a deep breath after chaos" — organised, welcoming, and confident without being cold.

## Design Principles
1. **Clarity over clutter** — every element earns its space; no decorative bloat
2. **Structure over overload** — clear grid, consistent spacing, logical visual flow
3. **Warmth over clinical** — soft photography, warm olive/gold tones, never sterile
4. **Restraint over drama** — subtle fade-ins only; no scale, no slide, no bounce

## Personality
- Colour: warm-neutral, muted olive/gold accent on cream/white backgrounds
- Typography: Artford Regular (logo "NEM"), Goldney Alt Slanted (logo "Life"), Lato + Montserrat (body/headings); clean, readable
- Layout: spacious, 12-column grid, max-width 1440px, generous vertical rhythm
- Motion: subtle only — fade-in on scroll, no slide/scale/bounce; smooth transitions; hover restores colour on muted images (home hero)
