# Ideal Customer Profile -- Carsa

## Primary segment: Local used car buyers

**Who:** Consumers in Teesside and North East England looking to buy a quality
used car. Typically 25-55, mix of first-time buyers and repeat purchasers.

**What they want:**
- A reliable used car at a fair price
- Transparent finance options they can understand
- Confidence that the car has been properly checked and prepared
- A dealership they can visit locally and trust

**What they worry about:**
- Hidden costs or fees appearing after the advertised price
- Buying a car with undisclosed problems
- Finance terms they do not fully understand
- Pressure to make a quick decision

**How they find Carsa:**
- Google search ("used cars Middlesbrough", "used [make] near me")
- Autotrader and other aggregator listings
- Word of mouth / repeat customers
- Local awareness (driving past stores)

**What matters in the website experience:**
- Fast, filterable vehicle search
- Clear pricing including finance monthly cost
- High-quality photos from multiple angles
- Easy way to enquire, book a test drive, or apply for finance
- Mobile-friendly (many browse on phone during commute or lunch)

## Secondary segment: Part-exchange customers

**Who:** Existing car owners looking to trade in their current vehicle as part
of purchasing from Carsa. Often already browsing stock and want to understand
what their current car is worth before committing.

**What they want:**
- Quick, accurate valuation of their current car
- Understanding of how part-exchange reduces their purchase price or finance
- A straightforward process without haggling

**Key pages:** `/part-exchange`, `/value-car`, vehicle detail pages (VDP)

## Out of scope audiences
- Trade buyers (Carsa is retail-facing)
- Lease customers (Carsa sells, does not lease)
- New car buyers (Carsa sells used vehicles only)
