# Carsa — Ideal Customer Profiles

## Segment 1: The First-Time Finance Buyer

**Role / Demographic:** 22-35, employed, may have limited credit history. Buying their first car on finance or upgrading from a starter car.

**Goals:**
- Understand whether they can afford a car on finance
- Get approved without damaging their credit score
- Find a reliable car within monthly payment budget

**Pain Points:**
- Confused by PCP vs HP — doesn't know which is right
- Anxious about credit checks and rejection
- Distrusts traditional dealers (pressure tactics, hidden fees)
- Worried about reliability of used cars

**Language they use:**
- "Can I afford this?"
- "What will my monthly payments be?"
- "Will this affect my credit score?"
- "Is this car reliable?"

**What they need from the site:**
- Soft credit check with instant, clear result
- Plain-English finance explanations
- Specific monthly payment figures upfront
- Warranty and inspection reassurance
- Low-commitment entry point (£39 reserve, fully refundable)

## Segment 2: The Pragmatic Upgrader

**Role / Demographic:** 30-50, family or professional. Currently owns a car, wants to part-exchange and upgrade. Price-conscious but values convenience.

**Goals:**
- Get a fair valuation on their current car
- Find a newer/better car without spending weekends at dealerships
- Complete as much as possible online, collect in person

**Pain Points:**
- Hates haggling and "what's your best price?" culture
- Wary of being lowballed on part-exchange
- Time-poor — doesn't want multiple dealership visits
- Suspicious of hidden costs surfacing at handover

**Language they use:**
- "What's my car worth?"
- "Is this price fair?"
- "Can I do this online?"
- "Are there any hidden fees?"

**What they need from the site:**
- Instant, transparent part-exchange valuation (locked for 7 days)
- Clear pricing vs. market average (the £700 saving claim)
- Reserve online, collect from nearest store
- Nationwide delivery option
- No-surprise pricing at every step

## Segment 3: The Peace-of-Mind Seeker

**Role / Demographic:** 35-60, buying for themselves or a family member. Less price-sensitive, more quality/trust-sensitive. May have been burned by a previous used car purchase.

**Goals:**
- Buy a used car that won't break down in 6 months
- Have protection/warranty if something goes wrong
- Deal with a reputable, professional retailer (not a backstreet garage)

**Pain Points:**
- Previous bad experience with unreliable used cars
- Doesn't trust "too good to be true" prices
- Wants someone to call if something goes wrong post-purchase
- Concerned about hidden mechanical issues

**Language they use:**
- "Is this car checked properly?"
- "What happens if something goes wrong?"
- "Can I extend the warranty?"
- "Are these guys legit?"

**What they need from the site:**
- Provenance check and multi-point inspection details
- Free 90-day warranty prominently displayed
- Extended warranty options (carsaCover — up to 48 months)
- Trustpilot reviews and FCA regulation badges
- Friendly, accessible support (WhatsApp, phone)
- Physical store locations for trust and test drives
